# Profile

Rudy Pena is a Staff Software Engineer in Austin, Texas focused on applied AI platforms, ML infrastructure, and developer experience.

His recent work sits at the intersection of AWS Bedrock AgentCore, Strands agents, FastAPI MCP services, LLM workflows, and Python/Node.js SDK tooling that helps teams move from prototype to useful product.

Before his AI platform work, Rudy spent years shipping developer-facing products, component libraries, web applications, and infrastructure at DTN, Anaconda, IBM, and Bitfusion.

The common thread in his work is making complicated technical systems feel approachable for the people who build on top of them.

Rudy is especially interested in practical AI systems, retrieval, evaluation loops, developer tools, product-minded engineering, and interfaces that make technical work easier to understand.
